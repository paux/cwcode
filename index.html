Code generator for CW training

by Stephan Paukner, OE3SPR

Usage: Use the generated, hourly 'codeid' in the URL, or choose your own arbitrary 'codeid' (any string) to share the same random text.

You can also define your own character pool by appending '&extrachars=' plus the desired characters to the URL. This could also be used to increase the occurence of certain letters or figures.
